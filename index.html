Places That You Must Visit

Prithvi Cafe

I had visited in December 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi Cafe" while you are on the main road. The restraunt has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicous, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celeberties on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disapointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep & happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi theater fest", which you must attend if possible.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my kids and they loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile

Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings
Enjoyable movie to watch with friends or family

I watched Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings months ago and I really liked it, the pacing and fighting scenes in the movie are very stunning and my brother also enjoyed it. The storyline is very hooking and I really liked the character development. I really reccomend watching this movie if you like a lot of fighting! This movies CGI (effects) and the weapons are so cool too! There are also many funny scenes and the MC (main character) is awfully humorous.
